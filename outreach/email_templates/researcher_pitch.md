## Subject: Cross-Linked LIHTC-REAC Dataset for Housing Quality Research

Dear Professor [Name],

I've built a cross-linked database that connects LIHTC tax credit projects to REAC physical inspection outcomes and management company performance — something that hasn't been available in a single structured dataset before.

**Dataset overview:**
- 54,102 LIHTC projects (1987–2023, all states)
- 33,772 REAC inspections linked to properties
- 4,556 management company profiles with portfolio-level REAC statistics
- 51,427 entity cross-links using address matching and property ID resolution

**Research questions this enables:**
- Do management companies with larger portfolios maintain higher or lower inspection scores?
- How do REAC outcomes differ between 4% and 9% LIHTC credit types?
- Which states have the worst-performing affordable housing stock, controlling for building age?
- Are properties managed by national firms inspected differently than locally-managed ones?

I built this using deterministic, rule-based extraction from three HUD public data sources — no LLM or ML in the core pipeline, so results are fully reproducible. Full methodology documentation is available.
